Bloom advocates for women and girl's rights as well as accessible contraceptives and sex education. We believe that everyone, woman or man, should be treated with the same amount of respect and dignity. We aim to educate people about sex and its consequences, as well as the different types of contraception. We work towards a gender-equal society and a world wherein women are given full and equal rights and the choice on what to do with their bodies. Be a part of bloom and help us achieve these goals!
